FAQ

Quick answers. Tap a question to expand.

How do we start?

I begin the project by asking you questions to expand upon your initial description. These include questions about any images you want added to websites, any color preferences, and maybe some screenshots of stream layouts so I can tailor the project to your style.

What do you deliver?

All of my services are done online, either through email or Stoat. All included files and apps pertaining to the project are sent to you, and I am more than happy to walk you through setting them up on your computer. Keep in mind, all websites I make are simply the files. I do not offer web hosting or domains. These are your responsibility.

How are changes handled?

The cost of your project depends on the scope of the project and the number of revisions or changes you make during the process. Larger projects will be more expensive. All payments are done preferably through Paypal, but other methods can be worked out if needed. Payment is required within a week of me agreeing to do your project.
