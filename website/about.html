Anime

I am incredibly picky with the animes I watch, and as such, have a small list.

In third place, Witch Hat Atelier is a great (and currently popular) anime. I love the manga, and the anime does an amazing job adapting it.

In fourth place, To Your Eternity is such a wonderful story. I love following characters as they grow and learn to understand others.

In fifth place, only because it is unfinished and on haitus, is Frieren Beyond Journeys End. I love having long lived character tropes, and 3 of my top 5 have this. Frieren is a beautiful story, and would be higher with more content. The manga is good too, but unfinished.

Books

As I have said, I love to read. Well written stories are places I can escape to and enjoy other worlds.

Unlike anime, I have read so many book series and genres that making a list is hard. However, I do have a top 5.

My favorite book series I have ever read goes to Ranger's Apprentice. It was a series I read growing up that taught me so much about morals and companionship.

Second place goes to Spice and Wolf. I absolutely ADORE the light novels and I see so many similarities between Lawrence and me.

Third place goes to Folk of the Air. This is an amazing fae-style fantasy series and Jude Duarte is my third favorite character out of any media ever.

Fourth place goes to Throne of Glass. Sarah J. Maas is one of my favorite authors and I love all of her series, but Throne of Glass is the best by far.

Fifth place was hard, but I ultimately chose the Beyonders trilogy by Brandon Mull. The story and setting are both so captivating, while also introducing characters that are impossible to forget.
